Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026
Your privacy is sacred to us. GodHealth stores all your personal data locally on your own device. We do not collect, sell or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Who We Are

GodHealth is operated by Robin, founder of GodHealth, based in Alkmaar, The Netherlands. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@godhealth.org.

2. What Data We Collect

GodHealth stores the following data locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage:

  • Your first name (entered during onboarding)
  • Your wake-up time and bedtime preferences
  • Your daily habit completion data
  • Your pillar scores (Temple, Soul, Spirit)
  • Your journal entries and reflections
  • Your weight and vitality tracking data
  • Your prayer journal and intercession list
  • Your training logs and workout history
  • Your 21-Day Arise Challenge progress
  • Your Covenant Circle partner codes

3. How We Use Your Data

All data is stored exclusively on your device and is used only to provide the GodHealth app experience. We have no access to your personal data stored in the app.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking cookies
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not store any data on our servers from the app
  • We do not sell your data — ever

5. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, your device registers with a push service. This is used only to send you daily Bible verses and habit reminders. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Payment Data

Payment processing is handled by our payment provider. GodHealth never stores your payment card details. All payment data is processed securely by our payment processor in accordance with PCI DSS standards.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a resident of the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data (use the Export function in the app)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@godhealth.org.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your app data at any time by using the Reset function in the app home screen. This permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.

9. Children's Privacy

GodHealth is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
