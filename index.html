The Three Pillars

🏛️ Temple (Physical) — "Know ye not that your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost?" (1 Cor. 6:19). Train daily. Eat biblical foods. Fast regularly. Sleep deeply.

🧠 Soul (Mental) — "Be ye transformed by the renewing of your mind." (Romans 12:2). Protect your dopamine. Journal. Build focus.

✝️ Spirit (God) — "They that wait upon the LORD shall renew their strength." (Isaiah 40:31). Prayer first. Scripture daily. Gratitude always.
