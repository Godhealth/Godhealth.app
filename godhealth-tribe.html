The Covenant Circle Principle

GodHealth is built on the Covenant Circle model. You do not find a random stranger for accountability. You bring one person — someone you personally know and trust. They bring one. That is how the circle grows.

This is how Jesus worked. He chose twelve people He knew personally. He invested deeply in them. He did not broadcast to thousands — He transformed twelve who then transformed the world.

"Where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them." — Matthew 18:20 (KJV)

"Two are better than one; because they have a good reward for their labour. For if they fall, the one will lift up his fellow."
Ecclesiastes 4:9–10 (KJV)

Ask your accountability partner to open GodHealth, go to their Connect tab, and share their 6-letter Covenant Code with you. Enter it below.

How the Covenant Code Works
1
You generate your unique code
Your 6-letter Covenant Code is generated automatically from your profile. It never changes. It identifies you uniquely within the GodHealth Covenant Circle.
2
Share it with someone you trust
Send your code via WhatsApp, message or in person to someone you want to walk this journey with. This is a person you personally invite — not a stranger.
3
They enter your code — you are connected
When they enter your code in their app, you appear as partners. When you enter their code, they appear in your circle. The covenant is established.
4
Weekly check-ins hold you accountable
Every week you and your partner share your scores, your struggles and your wins. Iron sharpens iron. You will not want to disappoint someone who is watching and praying for you.
"Confess your faults one to another, and pray one for another, that ye may be healed."
James 5:16 (KJV)
